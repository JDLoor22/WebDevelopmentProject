Discover the finest coffee beans from around the world, expertly roasted to perfection.

Special Blend

Also known as a "black" or "plain" coffee, it is the most basic form of coffee. It is made with hot water and ground coffee, without any other added ingredients.

Espresso Shot

A concentrated form of coffee obtained by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans. It serves as the base for many other coffee beverages.

Cappuccino

A beverage that combines equal parts espresso, hot milk, and milk foam. It is often sprinkled with cocoa or cinnamon on top.

Mocha

Also known as a "chocolate coffee," it is a blend of espresso, hot milk, chocolate, and sometimes topped with whipped cream.

Latte

Coffee made with espresso and a larger amount of hot milk, resulting in a smoother drink than cappuccino.

Americano

Made by adding hot water to espresso, producing a milder and less concentrated beverage.

Frappe

A cold beverage made with instant coffee, ice, and milk, often blended and topped with whipped cream.
